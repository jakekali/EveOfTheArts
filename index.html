Welcome To Evening of The Arts

  • Virtual Museum: 8:00pm
    Join Us for a Virtual Tour of our students art work. The artwork you are about to see has been created by Juniors and Seniors in Advanced Art and AP 2D Art and Design. Our students have used many different art techniques, including Acrylic Paint, Photo transfers, Collage, Photography, Vector Illustration, Photo Digital Manipulation, Pencil Drawing, and Mixed Media.
  • Concert: 8:10pm
    Join us at this very special event, where our students and faculty show their talents in Music and Drama.
  • Pegasus: 9:00pm
    The Pegasus is our school's award winning literary and art magazine. It showcases the very best of our students' work across multiple mediums to express their creativity and talent. Special thanks to Ms. Jaclyn Pahuskin, Faculty Advisor.
